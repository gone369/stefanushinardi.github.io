Where Ideas are Born

The Ideo Project

The ideo project is a medium for people to share their ideas. It allows ideas to bounce off and give birth to (hopefully) ground breaking ideas.
It's not so much a product rather than a manifesto. It takes form of web application, mobile application or even real world conversation.
The vision of this project is to able to capture process how great ideas are formed and try emulate the best condition for ideas to grow

"Eventually everything connects - people, ideas, objects. The quality of the connections is the key to quality per se." -Charles Eames

Current Projects

Currently, we are doing 3 main projects. The first one is a co-working space with 'coffee-shop' concept. Secondly, we are also building presence through Medium, which has the same vision as us. Lastly, we are building mobile app that allows people to track 'snippet' of their ideas during the day
